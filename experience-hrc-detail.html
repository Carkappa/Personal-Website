The Research Objective

After a hurricane, city planners, public health officials, and emergency responders need to assess physical disorder across large geographic areas. That assessment today is done manually — slow, expensive, and inconsistent.

This research trains computer vision models to detect disorder indicators in street-level imagery, creating a scalable alternative to field surveys that can support data-driven recovery decisions.

Model Training
